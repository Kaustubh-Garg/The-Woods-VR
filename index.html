About the Game

The Woods VR places the player in a dense, dark forest and an abandoned asylum overrun by zombies. Armed with a flashlight and a gun, the player must explore, survive, and manage fear inside an environment where the night never ends.

  • Immersive first-person VR experience.
  • Focus on atmosphere, tension, and environmental storytelling.
  • Hand-crafted terrain and layout (no procedural generation).
  • Asylum area filled with zombies instead of traditional cabins.
